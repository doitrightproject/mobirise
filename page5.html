INTERVIEWS

          Aside from Ate Merlinda, there were three interviewees who didn't want to show their face for the sake of their privacy. Kuya Albert, a fruit vendor, noticed that Plaridel Public Market is really dirty and full of trashes so he tries his best to always clean his stall and throw his garbage when collector comes. Lola (she didn't tell us her name), a buyer, agreed that the Public Market is unhygienic. She also said that trashes are an eyesore. It is very unpleasing to see. Monica, a student, said that there are a lot of trashes inside the Public Market. “Parang yung iba hindi na nagtatapon ng basura nila sa tamang trash can, they just litter around the place”, she added. She also stated that she’s willing to join projects inclined to cleanliness inside the Public Market.

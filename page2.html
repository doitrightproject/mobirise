DO IT RIGHT:

Proper Waste Management

It is common inside the public market to be messy, untidy and chaotic because it is the place where usually buy inexpensive fresh foods and goods instead of buying in the supermarket. If we want to buy vegetables, go to the market! If we want to buy meat, then go again to the market! That’s why the public market is always fully packed.

The public market has many trashes that make the place seem filthy. We observed that the half of the population, including the customers, in Plaridel Public Market doesn't seem to throw their trashes properly. They tend just to litter their trashes, varying from rotten vegetables, plastics, papers and even the insides of the fishes, around the vicinity that's why the place smells unpleasant and it is really unhygienic. Aside from the people throwing their litters around, the garbage collector also played a part on why the public market is still filled with a lot of junk. Most of the time, they do not collect the garbage in the right schedule. It is no doubt one of the dirtiest places that we can pass by.

Plaridel public market is where the Plaridelenos come and go. Tired of dirty surroundings inside the public market? We are encouraging everyone of these people to be a part of our Do it Right Project: Proper Waste Management that teaches each and every one of us to be responsible in throwing our trashes by putting it into the proper trash can and if they can't seem to find a trash bin, we advise them to throw it in their own trash can. By this simple act, we can make the Plaridel public market a lot better and a cleaner place.
